Solutions

Philotek provides turnkey, business driven IT solutions to the mid market and has helped hundreds of customers since it's inception. Philotek's proprietary approach to application and data classification has helped it craft solutions that are custom designed for the Commercial, Education, Government and Non-Profit marketplaces.

Whether you are looking to better understand your data or protect it Philotek can assist.

Below are just a few examples of where Philotek has helped businesses like yours achieve successful outcomes for some of their most difficult IT challenges.
Philotek saves Manufacturing Business Time with On Boarding and Off Boarding

Philotek worked with the business leaders of a well known manufacturing company to build out an onboarding and offboarding solution that tied directly into Active Directory allowing them to bring new employees on board 2 weeks faster than they previously could. This solution also helped secure the organization by providing the HR administrators a single click solution that disabled an employee's access to applications and data when they were no longer with the company.

“The Philotek team literally saved our HR Team 2 hours a day that we were spending adding user permissions...heck probably another 5 hours a week that we spend when someone no longer works with us as well...”

J.W AD Administrator
Cloud Migration that Helps Scale National Apartment REIT

Philotek's team levered the Azure Cloud to migrate over 215 servers and 100's of TB of data enabling one of the largest apartment based REIT's in the country to scale their IT resources at the same speed as they built or bought their communities. This saved the customer over $14,000/month and massive amounts of Admin time.

"Philotek took about 4 months to methodically move our data and servers. It was a great experience!”

PF IT Director
Philotek Helps Secure Telecom Company

Philotek worked hand in hand with one of the premier international telecommunications providers to design a secure topology that protected both their data as well as their customers.

"We've worked with Philotek for years in our data center so this was a natural extension. They did a great job helping architect our solution with state of the art technology"

JS Chief Security Officer & Chief Compliance Officer
Major School District Connects the Dots with Philotek

Philotek helped a major school district in Georgia design a system that allowed them to gather student learning data from multiple 3rd party systems and aggregate it into a central data lake so they could analyze their students' learning experience and progress.

"The information that we brought together and can now act on will help our students for years to come"

MK Executive Director of Innovation
