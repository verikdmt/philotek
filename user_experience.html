User Experience

Philotek helps organizations evaluate and improve their end users productivity. First, Philotek evaluates how end users interact and access applications. Philotek then helps make determinations on which applications make the most sense to migrate to SaaS or cloud based designs and which should remain as on premise solutions.
Next, Philotek helps design the appropriate security and connectivity solutions that provide the required level of protection while still enabling and improving access.

Then, Philotek looks at how employees access data and collaborate with one another either locally or remotely. We then discuss mobility and collaboration options with our customers to improve teamwork and productivity.

Philotek can help organizations manage the internal end user experience in a number of ways.

  • First, Philotek’s managed services team can take the mundane day to day help desk tasks off your organization's hands.
  • Secondly, Philotek can recommend systems that enable better visibility, accountability and security for your end users.
  • Lastly, Philotek can work with your team to enable your end users to work remotely and be more agile in the mobile business workplace.
